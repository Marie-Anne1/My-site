The little Mermaid

Summary

A long time ago in a kingdom, in the heart of the sea. There was a king sea, who had lived with his six daughters but he was more attached with the youngest one. Her name is Eva. One day, one of them celebrated her 18th and her father permit her to visit the surface, to see the world. The youngest wanted to go with her, the king didn't accept and promised her to see the world when she got 18th too.

Here arrived her birthday she reminded her dad about his promise.The king let her going to the surface, he told her to be careful and didn't stay near the humans. When she arrived to the surface: she saw the sun, the birds, the sky, everything was more awesome than in her dreams. She saw a prince like her doll, who celebrate his birthday on a ship . She was happy, she started singing.

Suddenly, there was a big storm, the ship was sinking. She swam fast to save the prince. He didn't see his savior. when he woke up, he saw another girl. Eva was angry and went back in the kingdom. She loved the prince a lot. She asked her fiend about, her friend gave her an advice about a magical person. when she went to the magical person,he gave her a potion to become human and to win the heart of the prince. When she arrived to the surface, she drank the potion. The prince took care of her,he really appreciate her. But he married with another girl. She was very sad. her family went back to the magical person, she gave her a dagger to kill the prince. Eva didn't do this and she went in the sky with her god mother.
